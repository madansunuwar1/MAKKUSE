Our Franchise

Join hands with Makkusé and bring the authentic taste of Nepali desserts to your community! We currently have two thriving franchise stores in Jhamsikhel, Lalitpur, and Lakeside, Pokhara.

If you’re passionate about preserving Nepal’s rich culinary heritage and want to be part of our mission to revive lost recipes, we invite you to open your own Makkusé franchise. Whether you're based in Nepal or abroad, we’ll provide all the guidance and support you need to ensure your success.

To explore this exciting opportunity and become a part of the Makkusé family, contact us today!

Let’s share the richness of Nepali flavors, one store at a time.
